Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Revaluator ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our services.

2. Data We Access

When you connect your bank account through our Open Banking integration (powered by Enable Banking), we may access:

  • Account information (account numbers, balances)
  • Transaction history
  • Account holder details

3. How We Use Data

We use this data solely for:

  • Personal financial analysis and tracking
  • Generating insights about your spending
  • No data is shared with third parties

4. Data Storage

Your banking data is processed locally and not stored on external servers. We do not retain your bank credentials.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Revoke bank access through your bank's settings
